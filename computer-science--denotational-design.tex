The meaning of an expression is a function of the meaning of its subexpressions.

(Semantic?) Model:
\begin{minted}[escapeinside=\\`\\`]{haskell}
  `$\mu$` :: Image `$\to$` (Location `$\to$` Color)
\end{minted}

maps a type to its meaning.
